Our Services

WHAT IS ANODISING AND WHY THIS WILL BE DONE ?

More Services

Aluminium and most of its Alloyes have a tendency to Corrode in the presence of Atmospheric Oxygen forming a thin Layer of Aluminium Oxide. This Corrosion is Prevented by Creating a Layer of Hard Oxide on the Aluminium Surface by an Electrolytic Process called Anodising. The thickness of the Anodising Layer varies from 5 to 30 Microns which not only prevent corrosion but also gives a better look to Aluminium. Anodising also forms an effective best coat for surface treatment,, which can be coloured to give an Attractive look.
